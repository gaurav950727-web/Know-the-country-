Overview

India, officially the Republic of India, is a South Asian country known for its diverse culture, rich history, and vibrant society. It is the world's second-most populous country and a rapidly growing economy.

Government

  • Type: Federal parliamentary democratic republic
  • Capital: New Delhi
  • President (head of state): President of India
  • Prime Minister (head of government): Prime Minister of India

Population

  • Approximate population: 1.4 billion (2025 estimate)
  • Major languages: Hindi, English, and numerous regional languages

Economy

  • GDP ranking: 5th largest economy globally by nominal GDP
  • Main sectors: Agriculture, manufacturing, services, IT and software services
  • Currency: Indian Rupee (INR)

Agriculture & Main Crops

  • Major crops: Rice, wheat, cotton, sugarcane, and pulses
  • Agriculture employs a significant portion of the population

Festivals

  • Diwali: Festival of Lights
  • Holi: Festival of Colors
  • Eid, Christmas, Navratri, Durga Puja, Pongal and many more celebrated across regions

Society & Culture

India is known for its cultural diversity, with various religions, languages, and traditions coexisting. It has a rich heritage of arts, music, dance, and cuisine that varies widely by region.
