Overview

Russia, the largest country in the world by land area, spans Eastern Europe and northern Asia. It has a rich history as a cultural and political powerhouse with diverse landscapes and peoples.

Government

  • Type: Federal semi-presidential republic
  • Capital: Moscow
  • President (head of state): President of Russia
  • Prime Minister (head of government): Prime Minister of Russia

Population

  • Approximate population: 145 million (2025 estimate)
  • Major languages: Russian

Economy

  • GDP ranking: 11th largest economy globally by nominal GDP
  • Main sectors: Energy, mining, manufacturing, agriculture
  • Currency: Russian Ruble (RUB)

Agriculture & Main Crops

  • Major crops: Wheat, barley, potatoes, sunflowers, sugar beets
  • Agriculture is a significant part of the economy

Festivals

  • New Year
  • Victory Day
  • Maslenitsa (Butter Week) and Orthodox Christmas

Society & Culture

Russia is known for its rich cultural contributions in literature, music, ballet, and visual arts. It is a country with vast ethnic diversity and a mixed heritage of European and Asian influences.
